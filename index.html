Discover Maine's Beauty

Maine is known for its beautiful ocean coastline, pristine crystal clear lakes, and thriving coastal towns. Mount Desert Island is the largest island off the coast of Maine and is home to Acadia National Park and Bar Harbor. With an area of 108 square miles and 64 miles of coastline alone being protected by the park many feel the best way to absorb our beautiful area is by water.

Take advantage of this by renting a paddle board or kayak and seeing this first hand! Witness the striking mountains of Acadia, the miles of untouched nature, the quaint lobstering villages, or simply grab a fishing rod and a bathing suit and go enjoy a sunny day!

Book your stand up paddle boards and kayaks today! There has been a lot of requests for these over the last few years and we will surely sell out so make sure to book early!
