Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your paddling adventure

Locations: Lake vs. Harbor - Which Should I Choose?

For families with children or those new to paddle boarding, we often recommend the lake for several reasons:

  • Warmer water temperatures
  • Calmer conditions
  • More protected environment

The harbor offers stunning scenic views and wildlife spotting opportunities but does have cooler water temperatures. We provide wetsuits for harbor paddling to ensure your comfort. Both locations are suitable for all skill levels, with minimal wave activity in protected areas.

Best Time for Paddle Boarding?

For the optimal paddling experience:

  • Morning sessions (before 11am) typically offer the calmest conditions
  • Evening sessions can provide beautiful sunset views
  • Afternoon winds are common but we stay in protected areas
  • For families with children, we strongly recommend morning bookings

Water Temperature Concerns

Maine's water temperatures vary by location and season:

  • Lakes warm up to comfortable temperatures by mid-summer
  • Harbor waters remain cool but are manageable with proper gear
  • We provide high-quality wetsuits and booties for harbor paddling
  • Wetsuit gear is available upon request for any location

Standing Requirements

Paddle boarding is versatile and adaptable to your comfort level:

  • Standing is optional - not required
  • Many paddlers start on their knees and gradually stand up
  • Sitting position is perfectly acceptable
  • Our boards are stable and suitable for all body types
  • Instructors can provide tips for your specific situation
